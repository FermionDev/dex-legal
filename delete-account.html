Delete Your Data

DEX stores all data locally on your device. There is no cloud account to close. You control deletion entirely.

Disconnect a connected bank (Plaid)

In DEX, open Banking → Auto-Import → Plaid and click Disconnect next to the institution. DEX revokes the Plaid access token and removes the item from local state.

Wipe all Plaid-imported data

In DEX, open Banking → Auto-Import → Plaid and click Wipe Plaid Data. This revokes every Plaid access token and removes every Plaid-sourced transaction from the local ledger.

Disconnect QuickBooks Online

In DEX, open Accounting → Integrations → QuickBooks and click Disconnect. You may also revoke access from your Intuit account settings.

Delete everything

To remove all DEX data from your device:

  • Quit DEX.
  • Delete the application's data directory (located inside the DEX install folder).
  • Optionally uninstall DEX through your operating system.
